<div className="text-base leading-relaxed">
  If you’re wondering who we are, let me fill you in. We’re Irys, the world’s first programmable datachain.
</div>

<div className="text-base leading-relaxed mt-4">
  <strong>Datachain</strong> means we’re an L1 that incentivizes storage.
</div>

<div className="text-base leading-relaxed mt-4">
  <strong>Programmable</strong> means that data on Irys is composable. On Irys, smart contracts can natively <strong>access</strong> (read), <strong>manipulate</strong> (validate, reference, or evolve), and <strong>write</strong> back to the storage layer (output)—all within a single protocol.
</div>

<div className="text-base leading-relaxed mt-4">
  Our private testnet is live as of August 30th, 2024, starting with support for permanent data uploads. Over the coming weeks, we’ll be rolling out the IrysVM, temporary data, and programmable data.
</div>
